Events

Stay connected with what is happening at Jimma Hiwot Berhan Church. From worship nights to community outreach, there is something for everyone.

Vibrant Community Life

Life at Jimma Hiwot Berhan Church is full of opportunities to connect, grow, and serve. Throughout the year, we host a wide variety of events designed to strengthen your faith, deepen your relationships, and engage our community.

From powerful worship nights and Bible conferences to fun-filled family days and community service projects, our events calendar reflects the vibrancy and diversity of our church family.

Whether you are looking for a place to bring your family, a space to grow in your faith, or an opportunity to make a difference in the community, you will find it here. We encourage you to mark your calendars, invite a friend, and come ready to experience God's goodness in a fresh way.

Annual Events Calendar

Our special recurring events throughout the year mark the rhythm of life at Jimma Hiwot Berhan Church.

Month Event Description Type
January New Year Prayer & Fasting Three days of corporate prayer and fasting to dedicate the new year to God and seek His direction. Spiritual
March Women's Retreat A weekend retreat for women featuring worship, teaching, prayer, and fellowship at a beautiful retreat center outside Jimma. Retreat
April Easter Celebrations Good Friday service, Easter sunrise service, and main Easter worship celebrating the resurrection of Christ. Worship
May Youth Conference Annual multi-day conference for youth ages 12-30 with guest speakers, worship, workshops, and fellowship activities. Youth
June VBS (Children's Camp) A fun-filled week of Bible stories, crafts, games, songs, and activities for children aged 4-12. Children
July Church Anniversary (July 20) Celebrating our founding with a special worship service, testimonies, anniversary meal, and vision casting for the year ahead. Celebration
September Family Day A special day for families with games, food, family devotion, and activities that strengthen family bonds. Family
October Harvest Thanksgiving A service of thanksgiving for God's provision, featuring special music, testimonies of gratitude, and a shared meal. Worship
November Community Outreach A month-long series of community service projects including food drives, clothing distribution, and health screenings. Outreach
December Christmas Celebration (Dec 25) Christmas worship service with choir performances, nativity drama, candlelight worship, and communal feast. Celebration

Host an Event at Jimma Hiwot Berhan Church

Our church facilities are available for weddings, conferences, seminars, workshops, community meetings, and other special events. We have a range of spaces to accommodate groups of various sizes, from intimate gatherings of 20 people to large conferences of up to 1,000 attendees.

Our facilities include the main sanctuary (seating 1,500), a fellowship hall (seating 200), a youth hall (seating 100), multiple classrooms, a kitchen, and outdoor grounds suitable for large events. Audio-visual equipment, sound systems, and projectors are available for use upon request.
