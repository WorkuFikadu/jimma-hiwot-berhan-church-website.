Welcome to Jimma Hiwot Berhan Church

A Family of Faith

Jimma Hiwot Berhan Church, meaning "Light of Life," is more than a church â€” we are a family bound together by the love of Christ. Founded in 1948 E.C in the vibrant city of Jimma, our congregation has grown from a small gathering of believers to a thriving community of over 4,500 members who support, encourage, and uplift one another through every season of life.

Rooted in God's Word

We believe that the Bible is the living Word of God and the foundation for everything we do. Through our Sunday worship services, midweek Bible studies, and small group fellowships, we dive deep into Scripture to understand God's purpose for our lives and to grow in wisdom, faith, and spiritual maturity.

Serving Our Community

Our commitment extends far beyond the church walls. Through our community outreach programs, we provide food assistance, educational support for children, health awareness campaigns, and vocational training for young adults. We believe that faith without action is incomplete, and we are called to be the hands and feet of Jesus in Jimma.

Walking in the Light of God's Grace

Pastor Abraham Tadesse

In this powerful message, Pastor Abraham explores what it means to walk daily in the grace of God. Drawing from Ephesians 2:8-9, he reminds us that grace is not something we earn but a gift freely given. Learn how embracing God's grace transforms our relationships, our work, and our purpose.

The Power of Persistent Prayer

Pastor Samuel Kebede

Pastor Samuel delivers an inspiring sermon on the importance of persistent prayer, using the parable of the persistent widow from Luke 18. He shares practical insights on how to maintain a vibrant prayer life, overcome spiritual dryness, and trust God's timing even when answers seem delayed.
