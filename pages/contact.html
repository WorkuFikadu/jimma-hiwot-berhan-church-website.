Contact Us

We would love to hear from you. Whether you have a question, a prayer request, or simply want to say hello, we are here for you.

At Jimma Hiwot Berhan Church, we believe that genuine relationships are at the heart of a healthy church. We are always delighted to hear from both our church family and those who are exploring faith or visiting for the first time. Whether you need spiritual guidance, practical assistance, information about our services and events, or simply someone to pray with you, our team is ready to help.

Please use the contact form below to send us a message, and a member of our pastoral or administrative team will respond to you within 48 hours. You can also reach us by phone, email, or by visiting us in person during office hours. We look forward to connecting with you!

Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome! We are excited to have you visit. When you arrive, our friendly greeters will welcome you at the door and help you find your way. You can stop by the Welcome Desk in the lobby for a special gift and information about the church. During the service, you will enjoy inspiring worship music, biblical teaching, and a warm community atmosphere. We have children's programs available during both Sunday services. Come as you are — there is no dress code. We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to settle in comfortably. For more details, visit our Worship Services page.
We are delighted that you are interested in becoming part of our church family! The membership process involves three steps: First, attend our "New Members' Orientation" class, which is a 4-session course held on Sunday afternoons covering our church's history, beliefs, values, and ministries. Second, meet with one of our pastors for a personal conversation about your faith journey. Third, participate in a membership ceremony during a Sunday service where you are officially welcomed into the congregation. To get started, simply fill out a membership interest card at the Welcome Desk or contact us at info@hiwotberhan.org.
Yes, pastoral care is one of our highest priorities. Our pastoral team is available for counseling, spiritual guidance, and prayer support for individuals, couples, and families. Whether you are facing a personal crisis, marital challenges, grief, or simply need someone to talk to and pray with, we are here for you. To schedule a confidential appointment with a pastor, please call the church office at +251 47 111 2234 or send an email to info@hiwotberhan.org. All pastoral conversations are strictly confidential.
Small groups and fellowship circles are a wonderful way to build deeper relationships and grow in your faith. We have several types of small groups including men's fellowship circles, women's Bible study groups, youth discipleship groups, married couples' fellowships, and mixed adult small groups. Most groups meet weekly or biweekly in members' homes or at the church. To find a group that fits your schedule and interests, visit the Welcome Desk, speak to any ministry leader, or email us at info@hiwotberhan.org. We will help you find the perfect fit!
Yes, we are committed to making our church accessible to everyone. Our main building has wheelchair ramps at all entrances, designated accessible seating areas in the sanctuary, and accessible restroom facilities. We have reserved accessible parking spaces near the main entrance. If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us in advance at +251 47 111 2233 so we can ensure you have the best possible experience. Our ushers and greeters are trained to assist visitors with mobility needs and will be happy to help you.
