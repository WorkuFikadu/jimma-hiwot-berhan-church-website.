Sermons

God's Word for Your Life — listen, watch, and be transformed by the power of biblical teaching.

"For the word of God is alive and active. Sharper than any double-edged sword, it penetrates even to dividing soul and spirit, joints and marrow; it judges the thoughts and attitudes of the heart."

— Hebrews 4:12

At Jimma Hiwot Berhan Church, we believe that the preaching and teaching of God's Word is central to the life of every believer. Each week, our pastors prepare messages that are deeply rooted in Scripture, culturally relevant, and practically applicable to the challenges and opportunities of everyday life. Whether you are exploring faith for the first time or have been walking with God for decades, you will find encouragement, conviction, and direction in our sermons.

We invite you to browse our sermon library, watch recent messages, or explore one of our sermon series. Each message is prayerfully prepared and delivered with the desire that you would not only hear the Word but be transformed by it. As Jesus said, "Everyone who hears these words of mine and puts them into practice is like a wise man who built his house on the rock" (Matthew 7:24).

Featured Sermon

Featured

The Transforming Power of Grace

Pastor Abraham Tadesse

Sunday, January 12, 2025

In this powerful featured message, Pastor Abraham explores the depths of God's grace and how it has the power to completely transform every area of our lives. Drawing from Ephesians 2 and the story of the prodigal son, he reveals how grace is not merely a theological concept but a living, active force that changes hearts, restores relationships, and empowers us to live beyond our circumstances.
